Case studies

Bridge App — B2B Merchant Platform

0→1 · 10K+ PARTNERS · 6 LOBs
+

The Problem

10,000+ distribution partners across six lines of business had no single platform to originate and service loans digitally — operations leaned heavily on offline and manual workflows, with no consistent way to track adoption or churn.

My Role

End-to-end product owner — roadmap, PRDs, and shipping — for the 0-to-1 launch of the Merchant App.

Key Decisions

  • Shipped 15+ features across all six LOBs against a single roadmap, rather than letting each LOB fragment into its own build
  • Built a GenAI document-extraction tool against Vahan APIs to automate RC validation, cutting manual review time
  • Ran user research and journey mapping with partners to diagnose churn drivers, translating findings into first-principles PRDs
  • Designed a Rewards & Loyalty program with personalized offers to drive retention, not just acquisition

Outcome

80% digital penetration across the partner base, 90% adoption and 70% MAU on the app, a 60% reduction in document-processing TAT (with 20% fewer errors), 30% lower partner churn, dealer revenue up 1.8X YoY, and DAU up 4X.

AI-Powered Collections — Agentic Voice & Bounce Prediction

0→1 · GENAI · RISK MODELING
+

The Problem

Collections relied on manual tele-calling at scale, with no early signal on which accounts were likely to bounce — agents were reacting after the fact rather than prioritizing before risk materialized.

My Role

End-to-end product owner for the AI-reimagined collections initiative, from voice automation to risk modeling.

Key Decisions

  • Delivered a 0→1 agentic AI voice infrastructure to automate tele-calling operations at scale
  • Built an EMI-bounce predictor using 360° customer profiling to forecast risk 15 days in advance
  • Engineered smart contact sequencing across omni-channel workflows to improve targeting precision

Outcome

₹2.34 Cr in annual cost savings (an 81% reduction) on tele-calling, ~20% of collection risk mitigated through early bounce prediction, and 40% better targeting with 99.99% reach accuracy.

B2C Customer App — UPI & Engagement

GTM · GROWTH · 10L+ USERS
+

The Problem

UPI was a new surface on the B2C app with no established adoption pattern, and organic growth on the broader app was flat without a clear engagement or referral strategy.

My Role

Product owner for UPI launch and the engagement/referral charter, including GTM strategy and metric design.

Key Decisions

  • Driving an IPL-linked UPI GTM campaign via Delhi Capitals sponsorship to accelerate adoption toward 1Mn+ transactions
  • Defined success metrics and ran structured A/B testing for the Engagement Charter, iterating on cohort-level data
  • Designed a referral program for a 10L+ user base to convert existing users into an acquisition channel

Outcome

UPI scaled to ₹10M daily GMV and 10K daily transactions within the first month at >95% success rate; organic installs up 30%; referral program drove ₹1.5 Cr monthly organic growth, contributing to a 33% revenue increase.
